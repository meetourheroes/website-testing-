About the Book

Heroes in action is a coloring book created to help children in Chaves County learn about first responders and public safty in a fun and friendly way. By highlighting local heroes and the tools they use, the book helps kids feel comfortable, build trust, and create positive connections with the emergency personnel who keep their community safe.

  • Fun coloring pages for all ages
  • Learn cool facts about first responders
  • Activities and tips to connect with local heroes

Why First Responders Matter

First responders keep us safe, help in emergencies, and make our communities better. This book helps kids learn, appreciate, and connect with these everyday heroes!
